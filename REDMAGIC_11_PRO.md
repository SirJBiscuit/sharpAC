# Redmagic 11 Pro Setup Guide

## 🎮 Perfect Device for This Project!

The **Redmagic 11 Pro** is an excellent choice for controlling your Sharp AC:
- ✅ **Built-in IR Blaster** - No external hardware needed!
- ✅ **Powerful Hardware** - Snapdragon 8 Elite processor
- ✅ **Large Display** - Easy to use remote interface
- ✅ **Gaming Phone** - Fast, responsive touch controls

## IR Blaster Location

**On Redmagic 11 Pro:**
- Located at the **top edge** of the phone
- Small dark window near the top center
- Between the front camera and edge

## Quick Setup (2 Minutes!)

### 1. Open the Web App

On your Redmagic 11 Pro, open Chrome and visit:
```
https://remote.cloudmc.online
```

### 2. Check Status

You should see:
- 🟢 **"Phone IR Ready"** with smartphone icon
- This confirms IR blaster is detected

### 3. Test It!

1. Point the **top edge** of your phone at the AC unit
2. Press the **Power** button in the app
3. Your AC should respond immediately!

## Positioning Guide

### Best Distance

**Optimal:** 1-3 meters
- Most reliable range
- Clear line of sight

**Maximum:** Up to 10 meters
- May need to point more carefully
- Ensure no obstacles

### Aiming Tips

1. **Point top edge** at AC unit
2. **AC receiver** is usually on front panel (look for dark window)
3. **Hold steady** while pressing button
4. **Wait for beep** from AC confirming signal

## Troubleshooting

### "Phone IR Ready" Not Showing

**Check Browser:**
- Use Chrome for Android (best support)
- Update to latest version
- Clear cache and reload

**Check Permissions:**
- Settings → Apps → Chrome
- Permissions → Allow all

### IR Works But AC Doesn't Respond

**Distance:**
- Move closer (1-2 meters)
- Ensure clear line of sight

**Aim:**
- Point directly at AC receiver
- Try different angles
- AC receiver is usually on front panel

**Brightness:**
- Avoid bright sunlight on AC receiver
- Close curtains if needed

### App Slow or Laggy

**Clear Cache:**
- Chrome → Settings → Privacy
- Clear browsing data
- Keep "Cached images and files" checked

**Close Background Apps:**
- Use Redmagic's Game Space
- Free up RAM
- Restart browser
